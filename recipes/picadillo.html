Picadillo

Mexican Picadillo is a traditional and comforting dish made with ground beef, vegetables, and a savory tomato-based sauce. It's hearty, flavorful, and incredibly versatile. Perfect served with rice, in tortillas, on tostadas, or as a taco filling. While modern versions may include olives or raisins, the classic recipe keeps it simple, highlighting the natural flavors of the meat and vegetables. It's an easy, everyday meal that brings warmth and satisfaction to the table.

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 450g ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled and diced
  • 75g peas
  • 4 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Blend the tomatoes with the garlic cloves and water; set aside.
  2. Place a large saucepan with the oil over the heat. Add the beef and onion and let them sauté.
  3. Add the vegetables and continue to sauté the preparation, stirring constantly, until the vegetables are lightly cooked.
  4. Pour the tomato and garlic sauce into the saucepan along with salt and pepper. Let the ground beef simmer for 15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro, if desired. Serve with rice, tortillas, or as a taco filling.
